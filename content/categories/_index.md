# General notes from research

GiveWell's current bar for funding is roughly \$105 / DALY-equivalent (about \$5,800 / life at 55 QALYs per child death averted) -- interventions cheaper than that generally get funded.

Rich-country health systems (NICE, ICER) pay up to \$50-100k / QALY; anything cheaper is "excellent" by their standards.

Coefficient Giving's global-health-and-wellbeing framework uses \$100,000 per DALY; a 1000x bar is therefore about \$100 per DALY. https://coefficientgiving.org/research/technical-updates-to-our-global-health-and-wellbeing-cause-prioritization-framework/
