# Internal Notes

These entries intentionally count contributions into the Laura and John Arnold Foundation / Arnold Ventures, not the foundation's grants out. Foundation grant announcements, Arnold Ventures grantee rows, and media-reported downstream grants are therefore excluded to avoid recording the same money twice.

The IRS/ProPublica parsed data exposed contribution-line amounts for 2011-2015 and 2019-2023, and Arnold Ventures' public filings confirmed 2022-2024 amounts and contributor names. Public copies for 2008-2010 and 2016-2018 appeared only as unparsed filings during this research pass, so no contribution amounts were included for those years rather than estimating from asset changes.

Excluded known items: the 2013 Head Start emergency support was treated as a foundation downstream grant or pledge and already covered by the 2013 foundation inflow; Arnold Ventures / Action Now Inc. 501(c)(4) and political advocacy spending was excluded under project policy; pre-foundation KIPP pledge references were excluded because public sources found did not pin an actual transfer separate from later foundation giving.
