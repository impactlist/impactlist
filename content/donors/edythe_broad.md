# Internal Notes

Edythe Broad is living as of the June 12, 2026 research pass. The Broad's official about page says Edythe continues the work of The Broad after Eli Broad's death and lists Eli as deceased on April 30, 2021: https://www.thebroad.org/about

Exact month/day of birth was not found in public sources; Wikipedia and other profiles give only 1936, so `birthDate` uses the year-start placeholder 1936-01-01.

Net worth uses the latest reliable public Forbes figure found for the Broad household before Eli Broad's death ($6.7B, 2019). I did not find a current Forbes/Bloomberg Edythe-specific figure during this pass, so this should be replaced if a current individual estimate becomes available.

Eli Broad is deceased and no Eli donor file is created. Broad household / Broad-vehicle rows in `content/donations/edythe_broad.md` are recorded at Edythe's assumed 50% share, with the full filed or public face amount preserved in each row note. This differs from spouse-collapse rows for living spouses: the purpose here is to add the living partner without crediting her for Eli's full share.

- Broad Art Foundation (EIN 95-4664939): art foundation and lending-library vehicle.
- The Broad (EIN 27-3032164): the downtown Los Angeles contemporary art museum vehicle.
