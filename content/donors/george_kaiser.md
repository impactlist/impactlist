# Internal Notes

- Verified living as of April 2026 (approved as a new partner in the Oklahoma City Thunder ownership group, Tulsa World, April 2026).
- His giving vehicle, the George Kaiser Family Foundation (GKFF, EIN 73-1574370), is a Type I supporting organization of the Tulsa Community Foundation, so it files Form 990 (not 990-PF). Donation rows are the annual contributions received INTO GKFF: Part I line 1d on the pre-2008 form, Part VIII line 1h on the 2008+ form, fiscal year = calendar year.
- Kaiser is confirmed as the contributor by SEC Form 4/5 code-G gifts of BOK Financial shares dated in the matching fiscal years (footnoted "Gift to George Kaiser Family Foundation" in 2019 and 2021), by his Giving Pledge statements, and by press coverage; GKFF has no outside fundraising. Caveat: FY2023 and FY2024 e-file XML separate out contributions from related organizations (Tulsa Community Foundation transfers for Gathering Place and similar projects, ~$22-24M/yr), which are excluded from those rows. For FY2010-2022 only the line 1h total is publicly parsed, so those rows may include similar related-organization amounts (roughly 5% scale, unobservable). FY2002-2007 filings report all contributions as direct public support with indirect support blank.
- FY2000-2001 inflows are not recorded: GKFF has been tax-exempt since February 2000 but no public copy of those returns was found (Candid's PDF archive starts at FY2002). The FY2002 return shows beginning-of-year investment securities of about $82M at cost, so on the order of $100M of founding-era contributions are missing from the rows.
- Net worth: Forbes 2026 figure ($16B, world #177).
- No photo: no freely licensed image of Kaiser was found on Wikimedia Commons (only Gathering Place photos).
