# Internal Notes

Net worth uses a rounded current Forbes-style figure; public search snippets in this pass showed recent estimates around $17B, while older Forbes 400 profile text put him around $15B in 2023.

The 2014 Silicon Valley Community Foundation donor-advised-fund gift is included as a separate donation event. SEC Form 4 shows Jan Koum's Butterfly Trust transferred 7.5M Facebook Class A shares to SVCF on 2014-10-30 and relinquished voting/investment control over the donated shares. Wired's article summarizing the Chronicle Philanthropy 50 valued the gift at about $556M, and New Yorker/Chronicle reporting described it as a DAF at SVCF.

Reconciliation with later Koum Family Foundation inflows: SVCF Schedule I filings checked through FY2024 did not show grants to the Koum Family Foundation or EIN 47-5446562. KFF FY2016 Schedule B instead lists four noncash Facebook-stock contributions from Jan Koum, and KFF FY2017 Schedule B lists Facebook-stock contributions from Jan Koum and the Jan Koum Family Trust. That makes the SVCF DAF transfer additive rather than an apparent DAF-to-foundation transfer.
