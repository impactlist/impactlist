## Open verification item: possible inter-vehicle double-count

Both the Grantham Foundation for the Protection of the Environment (990-PF) and the Jeremy and Hannelore Grantham Environmental Trust (990) have their contributions-received recorded as Jeremy's donations. If either vehicle granted money to the other, the receiving vehicle's contribution total for that year would double-count money already recorded entering the first vehicle. This could not be ruled in or out from ProPublica's parsed pages in this session: the Foundation's Schedule B contributor details are unavailable for the recorded FY2019–FY2021 rows, and the Trust's Schedule I grantee list is not exposed on the parsed filing pages. Note also that several Foundation fiscal years visible on ProPublica (2016–2018, 2022–2024, with contributions from ~$0.3M to ~$35M) are deliberately not recorded as rows; resolving contributor identity for both questions requires the per-filing IRS XML.
