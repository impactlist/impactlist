# Internal Notes

Paulson's main charitable vehicle is the Paulson Family Foundation (EIN 26-3922995). Donation rows record Form
990-PF "contributions received" into that foundation, not downstream grants out to Harvard, NYU, Central Park,
Hebrew University, Success Academy, or other recipients. Those downstream grants inform the foundation recipient's
category split instead.

The Paulson Family Foundation filing page lists John Paulson as an officer/director in the years used here; public
Schedule B detail does not provide a cleaner itemization of contributors. Any household share from Jenny Zaharia
Paulson is credited to John Paulson under the repository's one-file/default-public-giver convention because she is
not being added as a separate donor.
