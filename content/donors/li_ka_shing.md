- Verified alive before adding (the list excludes deceased people): he made a public appearance on 20 May 2026, aged 97, launching a Li Ka Shing Foundation liver-cancer treatment subsidy program ([Caixin](https://www.caixinglobal.com/2026-05-20/li-ka-shing-makes-rare-public-appearance-for-cancer-charity-initiative-102445979.html)). - `birthDate` follows Wikipedia (29 July 1928). Some sources (e.g. Britannica) give 13 June 1928; the July date is his official/registered birthday.
- `netWorth` is the Forbes 2026 Hong Kong Rich List figure of US$45.1B (February 2026, [SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/business/companies/article/3343353/superman-li-ka-shing-tops-hong-kongs-billionaire-list-us451-billion-forbes)); press citing Forbes real-time put him near US$48B in May 2026.
- Essentially all of his giving flows through the Li Ka Shing Foundation (established 1980), which states it never accepts external funds — he is its sole funder.
