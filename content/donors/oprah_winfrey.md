# Internal Notes

- **Living status verified**: Winfrey is living as of 2026; current biographical and financial profiles describe her ongoing media and philanthropic activity.
- **netWorth**: rounded to $3.0B from current public financial profiles that put her fortune at more than $3B.
- **Vehicle accounting**: grants out of the Oprah Winfrey Charitable Foundation are not itemized. The donation file records observable annual contributions received by OWCF after the 2012 education-giving floor, then uses the OWCF recipient split to model downstream grantmaking.
- **Education aggregate**: the $400M row is a conservative cumulative floor for Winfrey's education giving through 2012, based on reporting that she had given about $400M to educational causes by then. It subsumes pre-2013 education gifts through her direct giving, the Oprah Winfrey Foundation, Oprah's Angel Network, and OWCF.
- **Excluded / unresolved**: the 2024 WW International share donation to the Smithsonian is excluded pending proof of completed transfer and a stable valuation; reporting found during this pass described it inconsistently as roughly $3M to more than $18M and in some cases as an intended future donation. Political giving and campaign activity are outside scope.
