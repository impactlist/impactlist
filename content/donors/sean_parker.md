# Internal Notes

Net worth uses the Forbes figure cited in the current Wikipedia snapshot: about USD 3.0 billion as of May 2025. Sean Parker is married to Alexandra Lenas Parker; later Sean N. Parker Foundation filings list both Sean N. Parker and Alexandra Lenas Parker as contributing managers. This file credits the household foundation contributions to Sean Parker under the spouse/default public-face policy rather than creating a separate donor file for Alexandra Lenas Parker.

The Parker Foundation's site describes the foundation as launched in June 2015 through a USD 600 million gift by Sean and Alexandra Parker. The foundation had already been filing as the Sean N. Parker Foundation since the 2011 tax year, and its 990-PF contribution rows show USD 366,882,039 of cumulative 2011-2014 contributions, USD 1,194,053 in FY2015, USD 49,772,259 in FY2016, USD 0 in FY2017 and FY2018, and USD 0 in FY2024. Because the filings do not show a completed USD 600 million incremental contribution at launch, the donations file records the observable annual inflows and does not use a totalDonated override or a separate launch-capitalization row.
