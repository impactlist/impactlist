# Internal Notes

ADF is treated as Aliko Dangote's private charitable foundation. Its official foundation pages say it was incorporated
in 1994, that its mission is strategic investment in health and wellbeing, education, and economic empowerment, and
that its primary focus is child nutrition with wraparound health, education, empowerment, and disaster-relief work.

No public ADF accounts with grant dollars by program were found. The split is therefore approximate and program-based:
global-health 0.50 for the stated primary focus on child nutrition, community management of acute malnutrition, WASH,
health-system strengthening, polio/routine immunization, and other health work; global-development 0.20 for economic
empowerment and women's microgrants; education 0.20 for university and student-housing support; disaster-relief 0.10
for food intervention and humanitarian-relief examples including Jos crisis support and World Food Programme flood
relief. Sources: https://www.dangote.com/foundation/ and https://www.dangote.com/foundation/about-foundation/.
