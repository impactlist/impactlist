# Internal Notes

Arcadia's official grants page says it helps people record cultural heritage, conserve and restore nature, and promote open access to knowledge; its Who We Are page says it was founded by Lisbet Rausing and Peter Baldwin, funded by family trusts, and makes grants through Arcadia Philanthropic Trust, a charitable trust in Liechtenstein. Public inflows into the family trusts / Liechtenstein trust are not observable, so Lisbet's donation file uses Arcadia's cumulative grant awards as the conservative floor and does not itemize downstream grants.

Category split is based on Arcadia's May 2026 360Giving CSV linked from https://arcadiafund.org.uk/grants-awarded. The file has 498 grants totaling US$1,333,778,093.82: Nature US$614,640,308.41, Culture US$388,495,749.35, Open Access US$208,634,459.00, and Discretionary US$122,007,577.06. The rounded split maps Nature to `environmental`, Culture plus major cultural discretionary grants to `arts-culture`, Open Access to `science-tech`, and the remaining discretionary portfolio mostly to education, human-rights, and global-health.
