# Internal Notes

Category split is based on Form 990 filings for the Billi and Bernie Marcus Foundation. The FY2023 and FY2024 XML grant schedule shows Jewish Federation of Greater Atlanta as the identifiable grant recipient, and 990 officer listings identify Bernie Marcus as president and Billi Marcus as vice president. Jewish Federation grantmaking is treated mostly as religious/Jewish communal support with a local-community component for Atlanta-area social services and community infrastructure.
