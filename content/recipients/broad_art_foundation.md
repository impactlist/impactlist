# Internal Notes

The Broad Art Foundation is the Broads' dedicated contemporary-art foundation and lending-library vehicle (EIN 95-4664939). ProPublica classifies it under Arts, Culture and Humanities / Art Museums: https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/954664939

The Broad's official about page describes the Broad Art Foundation's lending library as holding more than 2,000 works and having made more than 9,000 loans to over 500 museums and galleries worldwide: https://www.thebroad.org/about
