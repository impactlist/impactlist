# Internal Notes

Recipient for Broad Foundation / Eli and Edythe Broad Foundation inflows and for the conservative pre-2011 Broad-vehicle aggregate where older upstream filings were inaccessible. ProPublica identifies the Broad Foundation as a tax-deductible private independent foundation (EIN 95-4686318): https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/954686318

Category split is approximate. The official Broad about page frames the Broad Foundations as advancing entrepreneurship for the public good in education, science, and the arts, and says the foundations created The Broad Center, the Broad Institute, and The Broad: https://www.thebroad.org/about

- education 0.35: K-12/public-education reform was a central Broad Foundation program; the Eli Broad page summarizes roughly $650M of education grants since launch: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eli_Broad#Philanthropy_and_civic_engagement
- science-tech 0.30 and health-medicine 0.15: Broad Institute/genomics and stem-cell/regenerative-medicine grants dominate the public science/medical portfolio. The Broad Institute public summary says The Broad Foundation (Eli and Edythe Broad) had provided $700M to the institute as of February 2014: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Broad_Institute#Funding
- arts-culture 0.15: public Broad Foundation/Broads commitments include LACMA, MOCA, MSU Broad Art Museum, UCLA Broad Art Center, LA Opera, and other arts grants. Separate Broad Art Foundation and The Broad museum recipient rows capture the dedicated arts vehicles.
- institutions 0.05: leadership-development and institutional-capacity work, including the Broad Center and related public-school-system leadership programs.
