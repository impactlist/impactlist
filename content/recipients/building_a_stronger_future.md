# Internal Notes

Building A Stronger Future Inc. (EIN 87-2995455) filed a 2021 Form 990 showing $3,000,000 of contributions received and $400,000 of grants paid. Its mission text says it supports journalists, scholars, policy research, media outlets, and educational programs, with a primary focus on risks from emergent technology such as pandemics caused by biological weapons, while also supporting functioning institutions and the free flow of ideas. Its 2021 grant schedule lists $300,000 to Institute for Progress and Altruism and $100,000 to Center for American Progress. The 45% pandemics / 35% institutions / 20% science-tech split is an approximate mapping of that mission and grant schedule.
