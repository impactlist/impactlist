# Internal Notes

CIFF's 2024 annual report gives 2024 charitable grant payments by sector: Climate $260m, SRHR and Girl Capital $146m, Health and Nutrition $132m, and Cross Cutting $93m, total $631m. The split above maps Climate to `climate-change`, Health/Nutrition plus most SRHR to `global-health`, Cross Cutting to `global-development`, and a minority of SRHR/Girl Capital to `human-rights` for girls' rights, child marriage, and reproductive-rights work.
