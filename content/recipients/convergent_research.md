# Internal Notes

Nonprofit incubator of Focused Research Organizations (FROs) that build scientific tools and datasets outside academia. Portfolio skews toward foundational research infrastructure (science-tech), and the Griffin/Schmidt-funded FROs — Parallel Squared Technology Institute (single-cell proteomics) and EvE Bio (drug-protein interaction atlas) — target biomedical applications (health-medicine). Split approximates that mix.
