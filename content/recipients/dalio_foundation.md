# Internal Notes

Dalio Foundation Inc. (EIN 43-1965846) is the 501(c)(3) private foundation behind Dalio Philanthropies. The split is a rounded estimate based primarily on the FY2024 Form 990-PF grant schedule (IRS object 202513219349106086, ProPublica page https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/431965846), smoothed against Dalio Philanthropies' current focus areas and operating-program descriptions (https://www.daliophilanthropies.org/).

The FY2024 ordinary grant schedule totals about $50.9M across 214 grants. A coarse dollar-weighted classification of that schedule put education/youth first (~32%), then broad philanthropy/civic/institutional and other (~20%, including the $6.9M TisBest giving-program grant), arts/community (~16%), health/mental health (~15%), ocean/environment (~10%), and economic empowerment/poverty (~7%). Dalio Philanthropies' public site describes three primary programs: Dalio Education for Connecticut youth and education, Endless for digital access, and OceanX for ocean exploration/protection; it also lists economic empowerment, health and wellness, and arts/community as focus areas. The final fractions preserve the FY2024 ranking while giving some weight to the enduring OceanX and education programs that can be underrepresented in a single grant year.
