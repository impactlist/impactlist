# Internal Notes

Split is approximate because I could not find a public dollar-by-program grant table. It is based on the foundation's official funding areas and named large commitments. The Dietmar Hopp Stiftung says it supports projects in medicine, education, social affairs, youth sport, and climate protection in the Rhein-Neckar region, and its September 2025 anniversary article reports EUR 1.4B donated over 30 years. Source: https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/2025/09/12/30-jahre-fuer-die-menschen-in-der-metropolregion-dietmar-hopp-stiftung-feiert-jubilaeum/

- health-medicine 0.55: medicine is described as a central "Herzensangelegenheit"; named large grants include EUR 100M for the Heidelberg heart center, the Hopp Children's Cancer Center / KiTZ building and follow-on research, newborn-screening research and infrastructure, pediatric oncology, prevention, palliative care, and hospital equipment. Key sources: https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/2025/10/28/spatenstich-fuer-smartes-herzzentrum-mit-assoziiertem-forschungsinstitut/ and https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/2022/09/22/forschung-und-behandlung-fuer-krebskranke-kinder-unter-einem-dach/
- local-community 0.25: combines social affairs and youth-sport/community facilities. The foundation funds local social-service providers, dementia and hospice work, disability inclusion, sport clubs, and 19 alla hopp! exercise/community facilities built for EUR 45M and given to municipalities. Source: https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/der-stifter-die-stiftung/
- education 0.15: covers kindergartens, schools, universities, educational organizations, inclusive education, science education, and the 2026 "Bildung in Bewegung" program with up to EUR 30M available for schools. Source: https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/2026/02/24/dietmar-hopp-stiftung-bringt-bildung-in-bewegung/
- climate-change 0.05: the foundation created the Klimastiftung fuer Buerger and financed the EUR 47M KLIMA ARENA climate-learning center; climate protection is now listed as a funding area, but publicly visible climate grants are smaller than the medical and local/community portfolio. Source: https://dietmar-hopp-stiftung.de/foerderbereiche/klimaschutz/
