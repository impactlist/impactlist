# Internal Notes

This recipient is used only for the residual aggregate row in `content/donations/donald_bren.md`. It models the public lifetime-giving total after subtracting observable Donald L. Bren Foundation inflows, rather than itemizing downstream grants or already-counted foundation spending.

The official Donald Bren biography says his total philanthropic giving exceeds $2.1B and highlights 57,500 acres of permanently preserved open space, a $50M pledge to Irvine Ranch Conservancy, nearly $50M to Irvine Unified School District, more than 60 endowed distinguished professor chairs, the UC Irvine computer science school, UC Santa Barbara environmental science support, and Caltech space-solar/science work: https://www.donaldbren.com/. The aggregate split weights the open-space and conservation portfolio most heavily (environmental and local-community), then K-12/higher education, science and engineering research, medical research, and arts.

The row deliberately does not itemize the named downstream recipients in the biography, because many were paid through the Donald Bren Foundation, Donald Bren Charitable Trust, or Irvine Company-related channels whose original inflows are opaque.
