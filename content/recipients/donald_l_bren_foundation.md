# Internal Notes

The Donald L. Bren Foundation (EIN 95-4094426) was established by Donald Bren in 1988. Its official page describes signature programs in education, science and engineering, medical research, the arts, environmental conservation, and open-space stewardship: https://www.donaldbren.com/donald-bren-foundation/.

The split is based primarily on the foundation's observable 2023-2025 Form 990-PF grant schedules and the Caltech announcement tying the 2013 foundation-funded gift to the Space-based Solar Power Project. FY2023 grants were $8.56M to Caltech and $2.0M to Irvine Unified School District. FY2024 grants were $8.89M to Caltech, $250K to Hoag Hospital Foundation, and $50K to the Hoover Institution. FY2025 grants were $6.87M to Caltech and $150K to the Milken Institute. Caltech says Bren donated more than $100M to create the Space-based Solar Power Project, the first donations were made through the foundation in 2013, and the Brens have no financial stake in the project: https://www.caltech.edu/about/news/caltech-announces-breakthrough-100-million-gift-to-fund-space-based-solar-power-project. The Caltech-heavy recent portfolio is mapped mostly to science-tech, with a climate-change share for space-solar clean-energy technology, and smaller education, health, institutions, and local-community shares for IUSD, Hoag, Hoover, and Milken grants.
