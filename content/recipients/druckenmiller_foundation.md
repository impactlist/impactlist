# Internal Notes

Category split is based mainly on the foundation's FY ending 2024-11-30 IRS XML grant list (object ID 202502819349100320), plus public descriptions of the foundation as supporting medical research, education, and anti-poverty work. The 2024 filing lists $135.7M in grants, led by health/medicine grants to NYU Langone Medical Center ($40.45M), Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center ($20.1M), Foundation for Sarcoidosis Research ($10M), New York Stem Cell Foundation ($6M), and smaller medical institutions. Education/local-community grants included Blue Meridian Partners ($31.164M), Harlem Children's Zone ($14.5M), Bowdoin College ($5M), the Spence School ($1.55M), and smaller youth/education organizations. Smaller grants went to Environmental Defense Fund and Oceana, and to arts/culture institutions such as the American Museum of Natural History and Metropolitan Museum of Art. Fractions are rounded judgment weights from that grant-dollar mix rather than a complete multi-year grant audit.
