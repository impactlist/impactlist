# Internal Notes

Freedom Together Foundation is the current name of The JPB Foundation (EIN 90-0747216), Barbara Picower's main grantmaking vehicle.

The category split is an approximate grant-dollar weighting, not a program-count weighting. Freedom Together's Beyond the Grant appendix reports 2012-2023 grant dollars of $2.7247B: poverty $1.1485B, environment $953.1M, medical research $268.5M, emergency grants $119.7M, President's Fund $114.2M, non-program $106.3M, and sector support $14.4M. The FY2024 Form 990-PF XML grant schedule adds about $470.0M of grants and is materially more democracy/social-justice oriented, with large grants to Community Change, Voter Registration Project, Trusted Elections Fund, gender and reproductive-equity collaboratives, Blue Meridian Partners, Energy Efficiency for All, environmental justice funds, MIT/Picower Institute, and reproductive-health providers.

Mapping choices: the historical poverty program is split primarily across social-justice, local-community, housing, and a small education component; the environment program maps mostly to climate-change because JPB/Freedom Together grants emphasize climate, energy efficiency, and environmental justice; medical research and reproductive-health work map to health-medicine; democracy, civil society, movement infrastructure, and electoral resilience grants map to institutions and social-justice; civil/human-rights grants get a small human-rights allocation. The split is deliberately broad because this recipient represents a foundation-wide inflow rather than one downstream grant.

Sources: Freedom Together "Who We Are" and "What We Do" pages, Freedom Together Beyond the Grant appendix, ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer for EIN 90-0747216, and the FY2024 IRS XML grant schedule.
