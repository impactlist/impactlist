# Internal Notes

Category split is based primarily on Gatsby's 2025 accounts for the year ended 5 April 2025, using grant payments plus direct programme expenses. Plant science (GBP 18.45m) and neuroscience (GBP 23.69m) are mapped to science-tech. Africa programmes (GBP 18.466m) map to global-development. Science and engineering education plus general education/science outreach map to education. Arts grants map to arts-culture. Public policy grants map to institutions. Parkinson's and PRIME-Parkinson grants in the General section map to health-medicine. Small residual general grants map to other.

Source documents:

- Gatsby 2025 accounts from the Charity Commission register: https://register-of-charities.charitycommission.gov.uk/en/charity-search/-/charity-details/251988/accounts-and-annual-returns
- Gatsby focus-area descriptions: https://www.gatsby.org.uk/
