# Internal Notes

Private foundation (EIN 99-3717522, ruled February 2025) created by George Kaiser in late 2024 to hold and vote majority holdings of BOK Financial Corporation stock "to support charitable intentions", focused per its filings on equal opportunity and social justice for young children in Tulsa and northeastern Oklahoma and on civic vibrancy and inclusiveness in the Tulsa community - the same agenda as the George Kaiser Family Foundation. It has no grantmaking history yet (first filing covers only December 2024), so category fractions mirror george_kaiser_family_foundation.md; revisit once it files a full-year 990-PF with actual grants.
