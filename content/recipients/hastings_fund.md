# Internal Notes

The Hastings Fund is Reed Hastings and Patty Quillin's donor-advised fund at Silicon Valley Community Foundation. Treated as its own recipient rather than generic SVCF because the available evidence points to a donor-specific education focus: the 2016 fund was announced for children's education, its first reported grants were college-scholarship gifts to UNCF and the Hispanic Foundation of Silicon Valley, and AP/Chronicle described the fund as primarily supporting education organizations when reporting the 2024 $1.1B contribution.
