# Internal Notes

The split is based on the foundation's public grant pattern and the raw 2024 IRS XML grant list. The 2024 Form 990-PF (object 202513169349101496) lists $56.8M of grants, led by Princeton ($13M, education), New York University ($10M, education/film arts), Schwab Charitable Fund transfers ($15.5M, opaque DAF/other), NAACP Legal Defense & Educational Fund and ACLU ($6M combined, human-rights), Film Foundation and film-preservation grants (arts-culture), After School Matters and Chicago institutions (local community/education). Publicly reported multi-year grants include Princeton's Hobson College lead gift, NYU's Martin Scorsese Institute gift, After School Matters, and Obama Foundation support. Rounded fractions reflect that mix rather than one single filing year.
