# Internal Notes

Category split is an estimate for foundation inflows. Humanity United describes itself as focused on transforming harmful systems into pathways for peace, freedom, and justice, with portfolio areas including forced labor and human trafficking, safer labor migration, peacebuilding, innovative pathways for peace, and inclusive peace processes. Human rights receives the majority allocation because forced labor and trafficking are core issue areas; conflict mitigation receives a large minority allocation for peacebuilding; institutions captures systems-change and justice infrastructure.
