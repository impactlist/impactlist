# Internal Notes

This recipient represents the Kenneth and Anne Griffin Foundation (EIN 36-4747915), used for Griffin/Dias giving around 2009-2014. The donation file records money into or constructively into the foundation, not downstream public charity grants.

The category split is based on the two large public grants that were previously itemized downstream and are now collapsed into the foundation vehicle: $16M to Children's Memorial Hospital / Lurie Children's Hospital for the Kenneth and Anne Griffin Emergency Care Center (health-medicine) and $10M for the University of Chicago / Harvard early-childhood education study (education). That known $26M portfolio implies roughly 62% health-medicine and 38% education. The FY2013 ProPublica inflow row uses the same split because public grant detail for that year's contributions is incomplete in the local source set.

Sources: ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer for EIN 36-4747915 and FY2013 contributions received (https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/364747915), University of Chicago's $10M Griffin Foundation announcement (https://news.uchicago.edu/story/anne-and-kenneth-griffin-provide-10-million-multi-year-study-school-improvement), and Philanthropy News Digest's $16M Children's Memorial/Lurie gift report (https://philanthropynewsdigest.org/news/children-s-memorial-hospital-receives-16-million-gift).
