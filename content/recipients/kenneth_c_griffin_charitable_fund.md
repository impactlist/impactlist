# Internal Notes

This recipient represents the opaque Kenneth C. Griffin Charitable Fund / Griffin Catalyst grantmaking stream. The corresponding donation row records an aggregate floor for money Griffin must have put into the vehicle or otherwise committed through the platform; individual downstream public grants are not itemized as donor rows.

The split is based on the 55 source-backed downstream grant announcements that were converted out of `content/donations/ken_griffin.md`, totaling $1,002,250,000. Category-dollar totals before rounding were approximately:

- arts-culture $373.71M: museums and cultural institutions, led by Museum of Science and Industry ($135M), Field Museum ($52.5M), MoMA ($40M), AMNH ($40M), National Medal of Honor Museum ($30M with a small education component), Norton Museum ($20M), Vizcaya ($20M), MCA Chicago ($15M), National Constitution Center ($15M split with institutions), and science/cultural museums.
- education $307.94M: University of Chicago Economics and the 2022 UChicago component, Success Academy, Miami-Dade College, Chicago and Miami student internet/tutoring programs, University of Florida, Achieve Miami, New Classrooms, and education components of Navy SEAL Foundation and National Medal of Honor Museum grants.
- health-medicine $199.57M: Northwestern Medicine, Nicklaus Children's, Baptist Health, Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center, Mount Sinai Miami, Lurie Children's, Shirley Ryan AbilityLab, the health component of Convergent Research, Ronald McDonald House South Florida, and related medical research and care grants.
- local-community $57.03M: Chicago Park District, The Underline, Project HOOD, local-community components of Navy SEAL Foundation, Obama Foundation, Ronald McDonald House, Preservation Foundation of Palm Beach, and the Chicago departure-gift remainder.
- institutions $25.00M: University of Chicago Crime Lab and the civic-institution component of the National Constitution Center.
- science-tech $15.00M: the non-biomedical research-infrastructure component of Convergent Research.
- religious $10.00M: Fourth Presbyterian Church's 2022 departure-slate component.
- disaster-relief $5.00M: Miami Disaster Resilience Fund.
- housing $5.00M: Lotus House.
- environmental $3.50M: environmental component of the Preservation Foundation of Palm Beach gift.
- social-justice $0.50M: Obama Foundation component.
