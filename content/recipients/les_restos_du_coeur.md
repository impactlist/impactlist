# Internal Notes

Les Restos du Coeur is a French poverty-relief charity best known for food aid. Its 2023-2024 annual report describes large-scale food assistance as the core activity, plus street outreach and housing support, health and rights-access work, and child/family support including education-linked accompaniment. I classify the emergency gift mostly as local-community poverty relief, with smaller shares for housing, social-justice/access-to-rights, health-medicine, and education. Source: https://www.restosducoeur.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/ra-23_24-complet-pap_bd.pdf
