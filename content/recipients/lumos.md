# Internal Notes

Lumos is Rowling's international children's charity focused on ending child institutionalization and moving children toward safe family and community care. The largest share is human-rights/child-protection work, with global-development for poverty and care-system reform, plus education and health-medicine for the practical services and supports involved in deinstitutionalization.
