# Internal Notes

Lund Trust's About page says it supports work that greens people's lives in the UK, has given more than GBP107 million since 2002, is named for founder Lisbet Rausing's home city, and is funded by family trusts through a CAF trust account and Lund Philanthropic Trust in Liechtenstein. Public inflows into those vehicles are not observable, so Lisbet's donation file uses Lund's cumulative giving as a conservative floor and does not itemize downstream grants.

The category split is based primarily on Lund's April 2026 360Giving workbook linked from https://lundtrust.org.uk/about, which covers 230 post-2021 grants totaling about GBP67.8 million. A rough grant-dollar classification produced approximately 42% environmental / UK green-space work, 23% health (dominated by MSI Reproductive Choices and Sightsavers), 21% Ukraine emergency response, 9% education, and small arts/culture and local-community shares. The final split separates the health cluster into `global-health` for international reproductive-health and eye-care grants and `health-medicine` for UK medical/hospice grants.
