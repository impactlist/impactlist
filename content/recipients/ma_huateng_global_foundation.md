# Internal Notes

Public research found the 2016 personal Tencent-share transfer into Ma Huateng Global Foundation and Tencent's 2016 annual report shareholding note, but no public grant list, annual accounts, or program mission for Ma Huateng Global Foundation. Classified as `other: 1.0` as an opaque charitable vehicle rather than using Tencent Foundation's corporate grantmaking categories.

Tencent Foundation / Tencent Charity Foundation is a separate PRC non-public fundraising foundation created by Tencent in 2007. Tencent's 2016 annual report says Tencent Group and employees funded it and describes program areas including disaster relief, rural development, education, ecological conservation and cultural preservation, community development, and medical care. Those corporate/user/employee flows are excluded from Pony Ma donation rows.
