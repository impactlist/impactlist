# Internal Notes

The Milken Family Foundation was established by Lowell Milken and Michael Milken and is described as focused primarily on education and medical research. Its major public initiatives include the Milken Educator Awards, TAP/System for Teacher and Student Advancement, Milken Scholars, the Milken-UPenn Graduate School of Education competition, the Lowell Milken Center, and the Milken Archive of Jewish Music. The split therefore maps most of the portfolio to `education`, a smaller share to `health-medicine`, and the archive/music/culture work to `arts-culture`.

Sources checked include the foundation's public descriptions, Wikipedia's Milken Family Foundation summary, and ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer for EIN 95-4073646.
