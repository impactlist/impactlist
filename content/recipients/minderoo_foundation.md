# Internal Notes

Minderoo Foundation is treated as Andrew and Nicola Forrest's main philanthropic vehicle, including predecessor Australian Children's Trust/Walk Free activity. Donation rows record inflows to Minderoo, not grants out of Minderoo.

The category split is approximate because public ACNC/data.gov.au records disclose total grants and donations but not grant dollars by focus area. It uses Minderoo's current stated priorities and historical named initiatives. Minderoo's purpose page says it focuses on three key areas: communities, gender equality, and natural ecosystems, with impact missions for urgent challenges and existential threats. Its FY2024 ACNC purpose text describes early years, place-based communities, children and families, modern slavery, human rights, gender equality and gender-based violence, ocean and terrestrial habitats, biodiversity, climate change, plastic impacts on human health, and charity-sector support. Pro Bono Australia's 2019 gift announcement similarly named cancer research, early childhood development, ocean health, and eliminating modern slavery. I map this to environmental 0.25, human-rights 0.25, local-community 0.20, health-medicine 0.15, education 0.10, and climate-change 0.05.
