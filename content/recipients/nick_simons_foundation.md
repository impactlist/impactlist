# Internal Notes

The Nick Simons Foundation (EIN 20-3101239) is the memorial foundation for Jim and Marilyn Simons' son Nick, who drowned in Bali in 2003 after spending time in Nepal. Its 990-PF grant lists show its grantmaking flowing to the Nick Simons Institute in Nepal — which trains and supports skilled rural healthcare workers (anesthesia assistants, biomedical technicians, hospital support) across Nepal — directly and via its sister vehicle Nick Simons Foundation International (EIN 47-2836360, whose contributions per every observed Schedule B come from this foundation, so it is not a separate recipient). Developing-world health-system capacity building maps cleanly to global-health 1.0.
