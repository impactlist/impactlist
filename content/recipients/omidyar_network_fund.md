# Internal Notes

Category split is an estimate for unrestricted inflows into the Omidyar Network 501(c)(3) vehicle, not a reconstruction of downstream grants. Omidyar Network describes its current mission as bending the digital revolution toward shared power, prosperity, and possibility, with work on culture, governance, business, and technology. Its historical portfolio and spinoffs covered economic advancement, financial inclusion, property rights, governance and civic engagement, education, responsible technology, independent media, data rights, and some climate/equity work. The split weights economic development and institutions most heavily, with education, human rights, science/technology, and climate as secondary allocations. Sources: https://omidyar.com/ and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Omidyar_Network
