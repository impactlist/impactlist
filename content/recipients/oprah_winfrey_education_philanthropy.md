# Internal Notes

This recipient is used only for the aggregate floor for Oprah Winfrey's education giving through 2012. The education share covers U.S. and international school, college, and scholarship giving, including Morehouse scholarships. The global-development share reflects the unusually large South African component, especially the Oprah Winfrey Leadership Academy for Girls and related education support for disadvantaged girls.
