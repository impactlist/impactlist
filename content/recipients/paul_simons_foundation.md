# Internal Notes

The Paul Simons Foundation (EIN 13-3937693, Stony Brook NY) is the memorial foundation for Jim and Marilyn Simons' son Paul, who died in a 1996 cycling accident. Its 990-PF grant lists show essentially all grant dollars going to Avalon Park & Preserve / Avalon Nature Preserve Inc. (e.g. $6.29M of $6.39M in FY2016, $12.9M in CY2023), the ~130-acre public nature preserve in Stony Brook created in Paul's memory. A land preserve open to the public splits naturally between land/habitat conservation (environmental 0.50) and a free local recreational and community amenity (local-community 0.50).
