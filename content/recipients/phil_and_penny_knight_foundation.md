# Internal Notes

Legal name on ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer is Knight Foundation, Beaverton, Oregon, EIN 91-1791788. The display name here disambiguates it from the unrelated John S. and James L. Knight Foundation.

Category split is based on parsed 2020-2024 Form 990-PF grant schedules reviewed during the Phil Knight research pass. Paid grants over those five fiscal years were dominated by University of Oregon Foundation support for the Campus for Accelerating Scientific Impact / Knight Campus (~$523M, science-tech), OHSU/Providence/Stanford brain-resilience/Fanconi medical grants (~$147M, health-medicine), and Stanford Knight-Hennessy/Albina Head Start/SEI/youth-support grants (~$120M, education). Smaller observed grant pools went to 1803 Fund, Blue Meridian, St. Mary's Home for Boys, Phoenix Center, Portlaoise GAA, Brookings, and the Obama Foundation, mapped across local-community, social-justice, arts-culture, and institutions.

The foundation's publicly announced $400M commitment to 1803 Fund was not fully paid in the 2020-2024 grant schedules; only the FY2024 $8.5M paid grant was visible. Future 990s may warrant raising local-community/social-justice/housing if that commitment is paid out.
