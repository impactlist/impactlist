# Internal Notes

The official PMNRF pages say resources are used primarily for immediate relief to families affected by natural calamities, major accidents, and riots, and also to partially defray medical expenses such as heart surgery, kidney transplantation, cancer treatment, and acid attacks. The 70/30 split reflects that stated primary disaster-relief role plus the explicit medical-assistance role. Sources: https://pmnrf.gov.in/en/ and https://www.pmindia.gov.in/en/pms-funds/
