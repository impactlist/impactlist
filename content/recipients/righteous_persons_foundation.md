# Internal Notes

Righteous Persons Foundation is Steven Spielberg and Kate Capshaw's private foundation. The category split is based on its described historical focus and 2024 Form 990-PF grant schedule. The Schindler's List / Holocaust memory source says the foundation put more than $100M toward Holocaust remembrance and Jewish arts/culture by 2014: https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schindlers_Liste#Impulsgeber_f%C3%BCr_Holocaust-Zeitzeugnisse. The 2024 grant schedule is dominated by USC Shoah Foundation / Holocaust survivor and remembrance support, Jewish arts and culture organizations such as Jewish Story Partners, Reboot, Skirball Cultural Center, and Chronicle of a People Foundation, and social justice / combating antisemitism organizations such as Avodah, Bend the Arc, Jews United for Justice, T'ruah, and Western States Center. I assigned 40% human-rights for Holocaust remembrance/survivor support and anti-hate work, 30% arts-culture for Jewish film/culture/storytelling, 20% social-justice for justice/bridging/community-organizing grants, 5% religious for Jewish wisdom/community work, and 5% education for Shoah/Colby/educational components.
