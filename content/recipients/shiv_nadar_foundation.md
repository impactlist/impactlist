# Internal Notes

The category split is based on the Foundation's official fact file investment table, which lists total investment of Rs 12,577 crore / USD 1.508 billion across its institutions. Education initiatives include SSN Institutions, VidyaGyan, Shiv Nadar University Delhi NCR, Shiv Nadar University Chennai, Shiv Nadar School, and SHIKSHA, totaling Rs 6,481 crore, or about 52% of the stated total. Kiran Nadar Museum of Art is listed at Rs 5,720 crore, or about 45%. The remaining roughly 3% is unallocated by the fact-file rows and is assigned to other. Sources: https://www.shivnadarfoundation.org/who-we-are/fact-file and https://www.shivnadarfoundation.org/
