# Internal Notes

Simons Foundation International (SFI) is a Bermuda-registered charity created in 2011 and funded from Jim Simons' offshore Lord Jim Trust (Paradise Papers/ICIJ reporting; ~$8B of assets by 2017). It files no US Form 990, so the split rests on thinner evidence than usual: SFI's own statement of purpose ("advance the frontiers of research in mathematics and the basic sciences", grants "primarily to scientific institutions, providing general support" — sfi.org.bm) and its few publicly attributed grants, e.g. co-funding the 2024 $75M CUNY computational-science/AI gift with the Simons Foundation. science-tech 0.90 reflects that general-support-for-scientific-institutions core; education 0.10 covers the university-capacity/training edge of those gifts (faculty lines, bridge programs). Revisit if SFI publishes a grant list.
