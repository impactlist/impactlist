# Internal Notes

The split is based on public descriptions of Schwarzman's philanthropy and the foundation's recent Form 990-PF grant lists. Large recent grants include MIT's computing and AI building, Oxford's humanities center and Institute for Ethics in AI, the Frick Collection, Inner-City Scholarship Fund, Animal Medical Center, food assistance, and USA Track & Field Foundation. MIT-style AI/computing grants are split between AI capabilities and science/technology; Oxford and Frick grants drive the education and arts/culture shares.
