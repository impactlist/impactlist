# Internal Notes

Split is based on the foundation's stated purpose rather than a public grant table. The archived official site identifies Dietmar Hopp as founder and says the foundation critically accompanies the work of judges and prosecutors "zum Wohle der Justiz" and to preserve and promote legal culture. The German Wikipedia article, citing the archived official site and 2004 press, describes the purpose as legal-fact research on German criminal justice, publication of research, reform projects, democratic legal order, and more effective basic-rights protection in criminal proceedings.

- human-rights 0.70: protection of defendants and citizens from disproportionate state investigatory action, procedural rights, and individual basic-rights protection in criminal proceedings.
- institutions 0.30: legal research, reform proposals, and criminal-justice system improvement.
