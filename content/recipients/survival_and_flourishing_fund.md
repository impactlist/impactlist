# Notes

The Survival and Flourishing Fund (SFF) is a grantmaking vehicle (run via its "S-process") that funds organizations working to reduce global catastrophic and existential risks. Although donors such as Jaan Tallinn technically transfer funds directly to the SFF-recommended organizations, we model this giving as a donation to SFF with a category breakdown reflecting where its grants go.

The breakdown approximates the distribution of the [2025 S-Process recommendations](https://survivalandflourishing.fund/2025/recommendations). The large majority goes to AI-safety / AI-governance organizations (e.g., MIRI, FAR AI, Palisade, AI Futures Project, PIBBSS, CAIS Action Fund, RAND TASP, Lightcone). A smaller share goes to biosecurity (SecureDNA, SecureBio, Ovelle Bio) and to forecasting / epistemics / global-priorities work (Metaculus, Centre for Long-Term Resilience). The 72% / 15% / 8% / 5% split roughly buckets that public grant table. We weight it more toward AI risk than the closely related long-term-future-fund recipient to match SFF's actual grant mix.
