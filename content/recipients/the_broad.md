# Internal Notes

The Broad is the downtown Los Angeles contemporary-art museum founded by Eli and Edythe Broad (EIN 27-3032164). ProPublica classifies it under Arts, Culture and Humanities / Art Museums and lists Edythe Broad as a director in the latest extracted filing: https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/273032164

The Broad's official about page says the museum offers free general admission, holds the Broad collection, serves as headquarters of the Broad Art Foundation's lending library, and draws more than 900,000 visitors annually: https://www.thebroad.org/about
