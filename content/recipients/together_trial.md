# Internal Notes

The TOGETHER Trial was an international research consortium running clinical trials of repurposed-drug treatments for COVID-19 (e.g. fluvoxamine), run via McMaster University and partners in Brazil. The FTX Foundation press release described its grant as support for global expansion of the trial.

We categorize it as health-medicine (not pandemics) to match this repo's precedent for COVID clinical research — University of Cambridge COVID-19 Diagnostics and the Long Covid Research Initiative are both health-medicine. The pandemics category is a population-level catastrophe model (cost per microprobability of a pandemic that kills >10% of humanity) and explicitly excludes near-term outbreak treatment/response, which is what this trial funded.
