# Internal Notes

Aggregate recipient for the unitemized remainder (~$5.7M to ~29 organizations) of Ken Griffin's $130M June 2022 Chicago departure gifts, after the eleven individually reported recipients were itemized in his donations file. Crain's characterized the full slate as educational, cultural, medical, and civic organizations, with the Chicago Symphony Orchestra the only named member of this remainder; the category split mirrors that description and is necessarily approximate.
