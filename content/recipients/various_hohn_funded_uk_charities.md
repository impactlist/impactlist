# Internal Notes

This aggregate recipient is used only where TCI Fund Management accounts, Giving List reporting, or account-based reporting disclose charitable donations by Hohn or his investment-management company but do not name or split the recipient charities. The 2024 and 2025 TCI accounts specify that the unnamed recipients are other Charity Commission-registered charities; the 2023 Financial News account-based report gives only the total charitable donations for the year ended 28 February 2023; the 2026 Sunday Times Giving List gives a foundation-inflow total for CIFF, The CH Foundation, and LightEn Education Network but does not split the reconciliation residual across those vehicles. Classified as `other: 1.0` because no defensible program split was available.
