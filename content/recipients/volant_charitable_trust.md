# Internal Notes

OSCR describes Volant as supporting Scottish charities and organizations that help vulnerable groups affected by hardship or disadvantage, with emphasis on women and young people at risk; it also notes international projects, Gingerbread/single-parent support, and MS research through the Anne Rowling Clinic. The split reflects that current mission: mostly Scottish hardship/local-community work, with social-justice and human-rights shares for women/youth/single-parent vulnerability, health-medicine for MS and health-related grants, and global-development for international projects.
