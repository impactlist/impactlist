# Internal Notes

The split is based primarily on the foundation's own 2024 grants page and mission statements. The 2024 grant list totals roughly the same order as the FY2024 990-PF attached schedule and is dominated by land, wildlife, and biodiversity conservation grants such as African Parks Foundation of America, The Nature Conservancy, New Venture Fund - Andes Amazon Fund, The Conservation Fund, Re:wild, Frankfurt Zoological Society, The Wilderness Society, Trout Unlimited, and other land-trust and protected-area groups. The next largest cluster is science and biomedical translational research, including President and Fellows of Harvard College, Wyss Center for Bio and Neuro Engineering, Wyss Zurich Foundation, University of Bern, UCSF, and other university or medical-research grantees. Smaller but material shares go to arts institutions (Museum of Fine Arts and Foundation Beyeler), economic-policy and democracy/legal institutions (Center on Budget and Policy Priorities, Campaign Legal Center, financial-reform/lending groups, Washington Center for Equitable Growth), local food/housing/youth/community organizations, social-justice and reproductive-health groups, and conservation-leadership education. Sources: https://www.wyssfoundation.org/ and https://www.wyssfoundation.org/grants/
